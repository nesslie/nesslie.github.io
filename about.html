        Here is when I write out of character and give my genuine thoughts and explain the reasoning behind certain elements on this website. I absolutely despise the aesthetics of this website, and will definitely not add it to a portfolio. As much as I hated it, the dated website design stemmed from the fact that I doubt the Gamey Bird would have paid newfangled web designers to create a good looking website, or add mobile support (huge pain). Website development is expensive (which is why I used this as an opportunity to try my skills and make more money down the line). I tried writing in the typical old man's voice, and it really just didn’t come out right considering the events it was describing. So I decided to go for an entitled, annoying, hypocritical tone for all of the “articles” on here. Besides that, this was a pretty fun project to work on. Ideally I would’ve added much more content and articles but actual coding took up a lot more time than I thought it would. Hope you enjoy!
